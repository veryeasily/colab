Gainsboro, TN Land

"The Land" is a Colab-held property in Gainsboro, Tennessee. The Land serves as a space where the more theoretical aspects of intentional communities join the pragmatic focuses of trying projects and cultivating the property.

Existing Projects & Maintainence

Solar Panels Information
There is one small and one large solar panel currently on the land. There is also a solar panel and batteries and an alternator. When you arrive down at the land, you will see instructions for usage attached to the inside of the barn wall. Make sure to contact the tools manager if you have more complicated needs or questions.

Cob Oven Information

Oxy-Acetaline Torch Information
We have an oxy-actealine torch onsite, which can be used for welding and cutting metal. The land can always use more instalations and we welcome all sorts of sculptural and general artists output. Make sure to contact our tools coordinator before using, thanks!

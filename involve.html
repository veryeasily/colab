Connect With Us

Never been involved with CoLab before? Start by emailing our new projects contact, [Joey Fatone - colabtennessee@gmail.com].

For all matters regarding CoLab's annual conference email colabtennessee@gmail.com with attention to Michael Skirpan or Dylan Bargteil in the subject line.

If you're already involved in CoLab and are looking for specific information (documents, photos) or need help promoting an event or project, please contact our information/communication coordinator, [Cadet Ellesley - starkslack@dude.net].

If you're planning to go to the land and need information about arrival or equipment use/maintenance email our maintenance guy, [Micha Gorelick - janitor@colab.org]

What's Happening in CoLab

If you'd like to see what projects are alreagy in motion or what we've got in our outlook see our shared google doc of projects. If a project already has a lead and you need information, contact the lead or our new projects contact if you can't find their email. You can also add your name to the list of contributors if you'd like to commit time/effort/etc to the project and be included on future communications. If the project you're looking at doesn't have a lead, add your name and contact us to let us know you're interested in leading the planning.
